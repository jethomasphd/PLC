Covenant

This is

  • A porch light — steady, local, calm
  • Fathers helping fathers
  • Real talk, practical takeaways
  • Anonymous — no profiles, no accounts
  • Offline-first — the real work happens in person

This is not

  • A feed
  • Social media
  • A debate arena
  • Therapy
  • Politics

How to Post

  • Go to Thread and check the three affirmations
  • Enter the council passphrase — shared in person at meetups
  • Pick a type: Win, Struggle, Ask, Offer, or Reading
  • Write your message — 500 characters max
  • Hit Post — your browser solves a quick proof-of-work (~2 sec)
  • You get a Lantern Receipt — anonymous proof you posted

No account. No email. No sign-up. Come to a meetup, get the phrase.

The porch light stays on. It doesn't flash.

Show up. Hold steady. Bring it home.
