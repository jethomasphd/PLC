# Components (Design Direction)

## Navigation
Left: small badge (logo)
Right: 2–4 links max (About, Meetings, Code/Resources, Contact)
Primary CTA button: “Join the next council”

## Hero
- big headline (5–9 words)
- 1 short paragraph
- 1 primary CTA + 1 secondary link
- optional: subtle “lantern glow” radial behind logo/CTA using --accent-glow

## Sections
1) What it is (2–3 bullets)
2) How it works (simple steps)
3) Who it’s for (inclusive language)
4) Next meeting (date/time/location placeholder)
5) FAQ (short)

## Button styles
Primary: gold background, navy text
Secondary: transparent with cream border

## Cards
Dark surface background, thin border, soft shadow
Short copy only. No walls of text.

## Accessibility
High contrast by default.
Avoid placing cream text on gold backgrounds.
