# Porch Light Council — Media Kit (Repo Seed)

## What this is
Porch Light Council is a local, face-to-face meetup for fathers and father-figures.
It exists to strengthen families by strengthening fathers through community,
practical wisdom, accountability, and consistent presence.

No lectures. No posturing. No online debate.
Real men, real talk, practical takeaways. Then you go home and do the work.

**Core idea:** Keep the light on.

---

## Brand pillars
- **Steady:** calm confidence, not hype.
- **Neighborly:** warm, approachable, local.
- **Practical:** tools, habits, small wins, accountability.
- **Protective:** home, kids, partner, community.
- **Offline-first:** the point is showing up in person.

---

## Logo
Primary mark: Porch Light Council shield with lantern and “SANTA RITA RANCH”.
Use the logo as a badge of belonging, not a marketing gimmick.

Logo file (source):
- `/assets/logo/porch-light-council_santa-rita-ranch.png`

---

## Quick usage rules
- Prefer **dark background** (navy/ink) with **cream/gold** accents.
- Keep clear space around the logo (minimum: the height of the lantern flame).
- Don’t skew, stretch, add drop shadows, or apply gradients to the logo.
- Don’t place the logo on noisy photos unless a solid overlay is used.

## Who it’s for
Fathers, stepdads, granddads, foster dads, and father-figures.
Married, single, divorced, co-parenting.
Blue-collar, white-collar, and in-between—anyone committed to showing up for kids.

---

## Design intent (for the website)
The site should feel like:
- a porch light in the dark
- a badge on a work jacket
- a town-hall bulletin, not a startup landing page

Clean. Bold. Minimal motion. High contrast. Big type. Short lines.
