# Voice & Copy

## Voice
Plainspoken. Warm. Steady. Practical.
No jargon. No therapy-speak. No political performance.

Write like a good neighbor talking to you on the driveway at dusk.

## What we say a lot
- show up
- hold steady
- bring it home
- keep the light on
- after the shift
- before the scroll
- real talk, practical takeaways

## What we avoid
- “optimize your fatherhood”
- “unlock your potential”
- “thought leadership”
- “growth hacks”
- culture-war bait

---

## Boilerplate description (short)
Porch Light Council is a local dad meetup. Real talk, practical takeaways, and accountability—so we can show up for our kids, our partners, and our community.

## Boilerplate description (one line)
A local council for dads: show up, hold steady, bring it home.

## Hero copy options
1) **Keep the light on.**  
A local council for dads who want to show up and hold steady.

2) **After the shift. Before the scroll.**  
Come sit with other dads. No speeches. No fluff. Just real life.

3) **Good men. Good dads. Good neighbors.**  
Face-to-face, once a week. Practical. Honest. Local.
